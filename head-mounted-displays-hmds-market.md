<p><p>Head-mounted Displays (HMDs) can be categorized into three main types based on their market presence. Slide-on HMDs are add-on devices that can be attached to existing headwear, such as helmets or glasses. Discrete HMDs are standalone devices that are worn on the head and do not require any additional equipment. Integrated HMDs are built into specialized headwear, providing a seamless and all-in-one solution. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of users in the HMD market.</p></p>
