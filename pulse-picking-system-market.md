<p><p>Pulse Picking System market consists of two main types: Electro-optic Modulator and Acousto-optic Modulator. Electro-optic Modulators use the electro-optic effect to control the polarization and phase of light, allowing for high-speed modulation. Acousto-optic Modulators use acoustic waves to diffract light, enabling fast and efficient modulation. Both types are essential in applications requiring precise control and manipulation of laser pulses, such as in telecommunications, medical imaging, and scientific research. Each type offers unique advantages and is used based on specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
