<p><p>Dental bone graft materials market includes three main types: Allograft, Xenograft, and Synthetic. Allografts are bone graft materials harvested from a donor of the same species, typically human cadavers. Xenografts are materials sourced from animals, usually bovine or porcine sources. Synthetic materials are man-made substances created to mimic the characteristics of natural bone. Each type of material offers unique benefits and is used in dental procedures to promote bone growth and support implantation success.</p></p>
