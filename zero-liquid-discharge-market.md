ZLD helps companies adhere to strict environmental regulations, reduce their water footprint, and minimize the impact of their operations on the environment.</p><p>The Zero Liquid Discharge Market is experiencing rapid growth, with a projected CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing need for sustainable water management solutions, stringent regulations on industrial wastewater discharge, and rising awareness about water conservation. <p><p>Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) is a process where all wastewater is treated and recycled, leaving no discharge. There are two main types of ZLD systems: Conventional ZLD System which utilizes various methods like evaporation and crystallization to achieve ZLD, and Hybrid ZLD System which combines conventional methods with newer technologies like membrane filtration. Both systems are aimed at minimizing water wastage and environmental impact by treating and reusing all wastewater generated by industries or municipalities.</p></p>
