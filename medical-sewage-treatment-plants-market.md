<p><p>Medical sewage treatment plants involve different types of treatment stages to effectively manage and treat wastewater from healthcare facilities. The preliminary stage or pretreatment involves screening and removal of large debris. The primary treatment stage focuses on physical processes like sedimentation to remove suspended solids. The secondary treatment stage uses biological processes to further remove organic matter. The tertiary or advanced treatment stage includes advanced filtration and disinfection techniques to ensure safe and high-quality effluent discharge.</p></p>
