<p><p>Ship heat exchangers play a crucial role in maintaining the temperature of various fluids in marine vessels. There are two main types of heat exchangers commonly used in ships: shell and tube heat exchangers and plate heat exchangers. Shell and tube heat exchangers consist of tubes enclosed in a shell, allowing for efficient heat transfer between two fluids. On the other hand, plate heat exchangers use a series of plates to transfer heat, offering a more compact and lightweight solution for ships with space constraints.</p></p>
