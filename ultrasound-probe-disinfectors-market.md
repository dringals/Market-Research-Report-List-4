<p><strong>The Ultrasound Probe Disinfectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs)</li><li>High-Level Disinfection (HLD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound Probe Disinfectors Market Types include Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs) and High-Level Disinfection (HLD). LLDs/ILDs refer to disinfection methods that reduce the number of pathogenic microorganisms on a surface but may not eliminate all spores and viruses. HLD, on the other hand, involves processes that effectively eliminate or inactivate all microorganisms, including spores and viruses. The choice between LLDs/ILDs and HLD depends on the level of disinfection required for the specific ultrasound probe and its intended use.</p></p>
