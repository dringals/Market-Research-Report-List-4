<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Coordinate Measuring Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bridge Machinery</li><li>Horizontal Machinery</li><li>Articulated-Arm Machinery</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bridge machinery consists of a horizontal beam that moves along an X-axis, while the measuring probe moves vertically along a Z-axis, making it ideal for measuring large, heavy parts. Horizontal machinery operates with a horizontal arm that moves along an X-axis and Z-axis, providing flexibility for measuring various sized parts. Articulated-arm machinery uses a flexible arm with multiple joints for precise measurements on complex surfaces. Other types of CMM include portable and optical systems for specific measurement needs.</p></p>
