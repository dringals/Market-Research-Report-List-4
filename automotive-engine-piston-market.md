<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Piston Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trunk pistons</li><li>Crosshead pistons</li><li>Slipper pistons</li><li>Deflector pistons</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trunk pistons have a skirt that extends down towards the crankshaft, while crosshead pistons have a wrist pin located at the top of the piston. Slipper pistons have a short skirt that doesn't extend all the way around the piston, and deflector pistons have a shape that deflects the fuel-air mixture towards the spark plug for better combustion. Each type of piston is designed for specific engine configurations and purposes, catering to different needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
