<p><p>An Electrostatic Precipitator (ESP) is a device used to remove particles, such as dust and smoke, from a flowing gas using an electrostatic charge. <p><p>Electrostatic Precipitators (ESP) are used to remove particulate matter from industrial exhaust gases. There are three main types of ESP markets based on the cleaning method: wet type, dry type, and others. In wet type ESPs, the particles are removed using water or liquid sprays. Dry type ESPs use an electrical charge to attract and collect particles without the use of liquid. The "other" category includes advanced designs and hybrid systems that combine both wet and dry cleaning methods for more efficient particulate removal.</p></p>
