<p><ul><li>Openwell</li><li>Borewell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric well pumps are categorized into two main types based on their installation: openwell pumps and borewell pumps. Openwell pumps are installed in open wells, tanks, or sumps, while borewell pumps are installed in borewells drilled into the ground. Openwell pumps are more commonly used in shallow water sources, while borewell pumps are designed for deeper water sources. Both types of pumps are used to extract water efficiently for various purposes such as irrigation, industrial applications, and residential water supply.</p></p>
