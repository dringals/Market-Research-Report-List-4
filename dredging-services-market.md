<p><p>Dredging services market can be categorized into two main types: Hydraulic dredging and mechanical dredging. Hydraulic dredging involves the use of water pressure to dislodge sediment and debris from the bottom of water bodies, while mechanical dredging involves the use of machinery like dredgers and excavators to physically remove material. Both types of dredging have their own advantages and are used in various applications such as maintaining navigation channels, reclaiming land, and restoring ecosystems.</p></p>
