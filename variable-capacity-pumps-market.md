<p><p>Variable capacity pumps refer to pumps that have the ability to adjust their output flow rate according to the changing demands of the system they are operating in. There are two main types of variable capacity pumps: Variable Displacement Piston Pumps and Variable Displacement Vane Pumps. Variable Displacement Piston Pumps use pistons to vary the flow rate, while Variable Displacement Vane Pumps use vanes to achieve the same function. Both types of pumps are widely used in industries where variable flow rates are required for optimal performance.</p></p>
