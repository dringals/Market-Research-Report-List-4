<p><p>Refrigeration machinery and equipment market can be classified into three main types - compression refrigeration machine, absorption refrigeration machine, and steam jet refrigeration machine. Compression refrigeration machines work by compressing a gas to increase its temperature and then cooling it down to remove heat. Absorption refrigeration machines use a heat source to facilitate the cooling process, while steam jet refrigeration machines use steam to create a vacuum that lowers the temperature of the refrigerant gas. Each type offers unique benefits and is utilized in different applications.</p></p>
