<p><p>The motorized thresher market includes various types such as spike-tooth type, axial flow type, hammer mill type, wire-loop type, and others. Spike-tooth type threshers use rotating teeth to separate grains from the stalks. Axial flow type threshers utilize a flowing motion to separate grains efficiently. Hammer mill type threshers use a high-speed rotating drum with hammers to break down grain. Wire-loop type threshers use wire loops to separate grains. Other types may include innovative designs or combinations of existing technologies for threshing grains.</p></p>
