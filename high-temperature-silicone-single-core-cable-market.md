<p><p>High Temperature Silicone Single Core Cable market can be divided into two main types based on the construction of the cable: solid single core cable and strand single core cable. Solid single core cable consists of a single solid conductor, providing greater resistance to electrical interference. On the other hand, strand single core cable consists of multiple thin strands of wire twisted together, offering flexibility and durability. Both types are commonly used in high temperature applications where traditional cables may not withstand extreme conditions.</p></p>
