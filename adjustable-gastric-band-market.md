<p><p>An Adjustable Gastric Band is a medical device used for weight loss in patients with obesity. It is a type of bariatric surgery that involves placing an inflatable band around the top portion of the stomach, creating a smaller stomach pouch. This restricts the amount of food the stomach can hold, leading to feelings of fullness sooner and helping patients eat less and lose weight.</p><p>The Adjustable Gastric Band Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of obesity worldwide, coupled with the rising awareness about the health risks associated with obesity, is driving the growth of the market. Additionally, advancements in technology leading to the development of more efficient and safer adjustable gastric bands are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>In recent years, there has been a growing trend towards minimally invasive bariatric surgeries, including adjustable gastric banding, due to their benefits such as shorter recovery times, lesser post-operative pain, and reduced risk of complications.
