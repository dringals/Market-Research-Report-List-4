<p><p>Gasoline Engine Control Systems Market includes three main types of Electronic Control Units (ECUs) based on their processing power - 16-Bit, 32-Bit, and 64-Bit ECUs. 16-Bit ECUs are older technology and provide basic engine control functionalities. 32-Bit ECUs offer more advanced features and improved performance compared to 16-Bit ECUs. 64-Bit ECUs are the latest technology with the highest processing power, allowing for enhanced engine performance, efficiency, and advanced diagnostic capabilities. Each type of ECU caters to different needs and requirements in the gasoline engine control systems market.</p></p>
