<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas pump market is divided into two main types: Centrifugal pumps and Positive Displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps are the most commonly used in the industry, working by using a rotating impeller to create flow and increase pressure. Positive Displacement pumps, on the other hand, operate by trapping a fixed amount of fluid and then forcing it into the discharge pipe. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in the oil and gas industry by transferring fluids efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
