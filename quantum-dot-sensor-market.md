<p><strong>The Quantum Dot Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistance Strain Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resistance strain type quantum dot sensors work by measuring changes in electrical resistance caused by mechanical strain. These sensors are highly sensitive and can detect even small changes in strain. On the other hand, piezoresistive type quantum dot sensors rely on the piezoresistive effect, where the electrical resistance of the material changes in response to mechanical stress. Both types of sensors are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and healthcare for applications requiring precise and accurate measurements of mechanical strain.</p></p>
