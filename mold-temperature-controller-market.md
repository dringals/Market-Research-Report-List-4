<p><p>Mold temperature controllers are used in plastic injection molding to regulate the temperature of the mold in order to improve the quality of the final product. There are two main types of mold temperature controllers: water mold temperature controllers and oil mold temperature controllers. Water mold temperature controllers use water as the heating/cooling medium, while oil mold temperature controllers use oil. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, and the choice between the two depends on factors such as temperature range, precision, and cost.</p></p>
