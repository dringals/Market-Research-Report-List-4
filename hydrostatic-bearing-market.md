<p><p>Hydrostatic bearings are commonly categorized into three main types: Radial Bearing, Thrust Bearing, and Radial Thrust Bearing. Radial bearings support loads that are perpendicular to the shaft, while Thrust bearings support loads that are parallel to the shaft. Radial Thrust bearings, on the other hand, are designed to bear both radial and thrust loads simultaneously. Each type of hydrostatic bearing plays a crucial role in providing smooth and efficient operation for various industrial applications.</p></p>
