<p><p>Organic Coir Market Types include White Fiber and Brown Fiber. White Fiber refers to coir fibers that are extracted from the immature husks of coconuts, resulting in a finer and softer texture ideal for use in products such as mattresses and upholstery. Brown Fiber, on the other hand, is derived from mature coconut husks, which makes it more durable and suitable for applications like landscaping, erosion control, and gardening. Both types of coir fibers have unique characteristics and cater to different markets based on their qualities.</p></p>
