<p><p>Semiconductor abatement systems are essential for removing harmful gases generated during semiconductor manufacturing processes. There are four main types of abatement systems: combustion type, which uses high temperatures to break down gases; wet type, which uses water or other liquids to scrub gases; dry type, which uses adsorbents or filters to trap gases; and catalytic type, which uses catalysts to convert gases into harmless byproducts. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
