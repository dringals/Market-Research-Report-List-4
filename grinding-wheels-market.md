<p><p>Grinding wheels are classified based on the type of bond used in their manufacturing process. Resinoid bond grinding wheels are made with a resin-based binder, making them flexible and ideal for light grinding applications. Vitrified bond grinding wheels are made with a ceramic binder, providing high strength and heat resistance for heavy-duty grinding tasks. Both types offer different advantages based on the specific requirements of the grinding application, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
