<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Handheld VOC Monitor market consists of two main types of technologies - PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor. PID monitors use an ultraviolet lamp to ionize volatile organic compounds (VOCs), providing accurate measurements. On the other hand, Metal-oxide Semiconductor monitors detect VOCs by changes in electrical resistance. Both types of monitors are portable and handheld, making them convenient for on-the-go monitoring of air quality in various settings such as industrial workplaces, indoor spaces, and outdoor environments.</p></p>
