<p><p>Aviation glasses are available in two main market types: single layer and interlayer glasses. Single layer glasses are made of a single sheet of glass that provides basic protection from sunlight and glare. On the other hand, interlayer glasses consist of multiple layers of glass with a special film in between, offering advanced protection against UV rays, eye strain, and impact. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of consumers in the aviation industry.</p></p>
