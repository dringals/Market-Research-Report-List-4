<p><p>Automated Labeling Machine market consists of various types including Pressure Sensitive/Self-Adhesive Labelers, Stretch Sleeve/Shrink-Sleeve Labelers, and Glue-Based Labelers. Pressure Sensitive/Self-Adhesive Labelers apply labels to products by using a combination of pressure and adhesive. Stretch Sleeve/Shrink-Sleeve Labelers use heat to shrink a sleeve label around a product. Glue-Based Labelers apply labels to products using an adhesive. Each type of labeling machine offers unique benefits and is suitable for different packaging needs.</p></p>
