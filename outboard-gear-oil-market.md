<p><p>Outboard gear oil is classified into two main types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from crude oil and is less expensive than synthetic oil. It offers decent lubrication properties and is suitable for general boating applications. On the other hand, synthetic oil is man-made and offers superior lubrication and performance in extreme conditions. It is more expensive but provides longer-lasting protection for outboard engines. Both types of gear oil play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of outboard engines.</p></p>
