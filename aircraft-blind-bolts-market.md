<p><ul><li>Drive-Nut Blind Bolts</li><li>Pull Type Blind Bolts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft blind bolts market is segmented into two types: Drive-Nut Blind Bolts and Pull Type Blind Bolts. Drive-Nut Blind Bolts are installed by driving a nut into a pre-drilled hole, providing a fast and efficient installation process. Pull Type Blind Bolts feature a pull mechanism that expands the bolt when tightened, creating a strong and secure attachment. Both types are widely used in aircraft construction due to their ability to create reliable and durable connections in hard-to-reach areas.</p></p>
