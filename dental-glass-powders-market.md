<p><p>Dental glass powders are categorized into two main types: inert glass powders and reactive glass powders. Inert glass powders are chemically stable and do not react with other materials, making them ideal for use as fillers in dental materials. On the other hand, reactive glass powders can chemically bond with other substances, providing enhanced strength and durability to dental products. Both types of glass powders play a crucial role in the dental industry, offering unique benefits and properties for various applications.</p></p>
