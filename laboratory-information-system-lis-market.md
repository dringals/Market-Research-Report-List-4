The market for LIS can be categorized into two main types - On-premises LIS and Cloud-Based LIS. On-premises LIS refers to software installed and operated on the laboratory's own servers, while Cloud-Based LIS involves hosting the software on remote servers accessed through the internet. Both types offer unique benefits in terms of cost, control, and accessibility.</p></p>
