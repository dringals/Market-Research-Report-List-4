<p><ul><li>Large FOV</li><li>Medium FOV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart CBCT machines come in two main market types based on their field of view (FOV): Large FOV and Medium FOV. Large FOV machines offer a wider imaging area suitable for full mouth imaging, orthodontics, and dental implant planning. They are often preferred by specialist dental practices. Medium FOV machines provide a more focused imaging area, making them ideal for specific dental applications like endodontics and periodontics. Both types offer advanced imaging capabilities and innovative features tailored to meet the diverse needs of dental professionals.</p></p>
