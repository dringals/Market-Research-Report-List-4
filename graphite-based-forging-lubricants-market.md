<p><p>Graphite-based forging lubricants are used in both hot forging and cold forging processes. Hot forging lubricants are designed to withstand high temperatures and reduce friction during the shaping of metal parts at elevated temperatures. Cold forging lubricants, on the other hand, are used in the shaping of metal parts at room temperature or below. They help reduce wear on tools and improve the surface finish of the forged parts. Both types of lubricants play a crucial role in the forging industry by facilitating the shaping of metal components efficiently.</p></p>
