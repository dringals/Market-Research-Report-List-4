<p><p>Robotic Milking Systems (RMS) are automated systems that milk cows without human intervention. The market types for RMS include standalone units, multiple stall units, and rotary units. Standalone units are individual milking stations that can accommodate one cow at a time. Multiple stall units have multiple stalls for cows to queue up and be milked. Rotary units are large systems where cows are milked while they rotate around a central platform. Each type offers different capacities and efficiencies to meet the needs of dairy farmers.</p></p>
