AC/DC LED Lighting Driver ICs are designed to convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) to power LED lighting systems, while DC/DC LED Lighting Driver ICs regulate and control the DC power supply to the LEDs. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable LED lighting operations.</p></p>
