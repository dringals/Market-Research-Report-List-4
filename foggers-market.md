<p><p>Foggers are devices used for pest control, disinfection, and odor elimination. There are two main types of foggers on the market: Thermal Foggers and Cold Foggers. Thermal foggers use heat to vaporize liquid pesticides or disinfectants, creating a hot fog that penetrates hard-to-reach areas. Cold foggers, on the other hand, use high pressure to create a fine mist of droplets, ideal for indoor use as it does not damage sensitive materials. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries for effective pest management and sanitation.</p></p>
