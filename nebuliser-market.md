<p><p>Nebulisers are medical devices used to administer medication in the form of a mist that is inhaled into the lungs. There are three main types of nebulisers on the market: pneumatic nebulisers, ultrasonic nebulisers, and mesh nebulisers. Pneumatic nebulisers use compressed air to convert liquid medication into a mist. Ultrasonic nebulisers use ultrasonic waves to create the mist, while mesh nebulisers use a vibrating mesh to aerosolize the medication. Each type of nebuliser has its own unique advantages and is suitable for different medical conditions.</p></p>
