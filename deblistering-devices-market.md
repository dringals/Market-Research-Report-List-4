Semi-automatic machines partially automate the process by assisting operators in removing the medication but still require some manual intervention. Fully automatic deblistering devices are the most advanced, requiring minimal human intervention as they can automatically remove medication from blister packs at a high speed, increasing efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
