<p><p>The Large Hydro Turbine Market can be classified into four main types based on the design and operation of the turbines - Francis, Kaplan, Pelton, and Others. Francis turbines are efficient for medium head applications, Kaplan turbines are suitable for low head, high flow conditions, and Pelton turbines are ideal for high head, low flow conditions. The "Others" category refers to less common turbine designs like propeller or pump turbines. Each type has specific use cases and advantages depending on the water head and flow rate of the hydro power plant.</p></p>
