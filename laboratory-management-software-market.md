<p><p>Laboratory Management Software is available in three main market types: On-premise, Cloud-based, and Remotely hosted. On-premise systems require the software to be installed and maintained on site, providing full control and security but requiring more resources. Cloud-based systems store data and software on external servers accessed via the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. Remotely hosted solutions involve hosting and managing the software on third-party servers, providing accessibility and support without the need for on-site maintenance. Each type has its own advantages and considerations for laboratories to consider.</p></p>
