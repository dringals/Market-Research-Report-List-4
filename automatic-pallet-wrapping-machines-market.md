<p><p>Single-arm automatic pallet wrapping machines have one rotating arm that wraps the film around the pallet. These machines are ideal for wrapping pallets with unstable loads or limited space. Double-arm automatic pallet wrapping machines have two rotating arms that work simultaneously to wrap the pallet. These machines are more efficient and can wrap pallets at a faster rate compared to single-arm machines. Both types of machines are used in warehouses and distribution centers to streamline the pallet wrapping process and ensure secure shipments.</p></p>
