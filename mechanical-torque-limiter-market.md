<p><p>Mechanical torque limiters are devices that limit the amount of torque transmitted through a system to protect machinery from damage. There are several types of mechanical torque limiters available in the market, including ball torque limiters, friction torque limiters, pneumatic torque limiters, and push/pull torque limiters. Ball torque limiters use balls to transmit torque while allowing slippage when a predetermined torque is exceeded. Friction torque limiters use frictional forces to limit torque, while pneumatic torque limiters use air pressure. Push/pull torque limiters use springs to limit torque transmission.</p></p>
