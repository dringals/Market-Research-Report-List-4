<p><ul><li>Shaft Mounted Reducers</li><li>In-Line/Concentric Reducers</li><li>Off-Set/Parallel Reducers</li><li>Right Angle Reducers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enclosed gearing market types include shaft mounted reducers, in-line/concentric reducers, off-set/parallel reducers, and right angle reducers. Shaft mounted reducers are directly mounted on a shaft, in-line/concentric reducers have the input and output shafts in line, off-set/parallel reducers have shafts that are not in line, and right angle reducers change the direction of power transmission by 90 degrees. Other markets in enclosed gearing include special purpose reducers, custom designed solutions, and hybrid systems combining multiple types of reducers for specific applications.</p></p>
