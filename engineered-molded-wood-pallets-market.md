Nestable pallets are designed to nest together when empty, saving space during storage and transportation. They are ideal for industries with limited storage space. Rackable pallets, on the other hand, are designed with strength and durability in mind, making them suitable for use in racking systems. These pallets can withstand heavy loads and are commonly used in warehouse and distribution settings where products need to be stored on racks.</p></p>
