<p><p>Motorcycle disc brakes come in two main types: Mechanical Disc Brakes and Hydraulic Disc Brakes. Mechanical disc brakes use a cable to activate the brake calipers, while hydraulic disc brakes use fluid to transfer force from the brake lever to the calipers. Hydraulic disc brakes are generally more powerful and offer better modulation compared to mechanical disc brakes. Both types are popular in the motorcycle industry and offer distinct advantages depending on the rider's preferences and needs.</p></p>
