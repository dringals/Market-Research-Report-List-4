<p><p>Selenium sputtering targets are available in two main market types: rotary type and non-rotatable type. Rotary type targets are designed to rotate during the sputtering process, which can help improve coating uniformity and target utilization. Non-rotatable type targets, on the other hand, do not rotate during sputtering. They are usually more cost-effective and simpler to operate. Both types of selenium sputtering targets are widely used in various industries for thin film deposition applications.</p></p>
