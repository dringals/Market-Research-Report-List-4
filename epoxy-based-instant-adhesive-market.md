<p><p>Epoxy-based instant adhesives are available in two main types: Cold Cure Adhesive and Heat Cure Adhesive. Cold Cure Adhesive is designed to cure at room temperature, making it suitable for applications where heat cannot be used. On the other hand, Heat Cure Adhesive requires heat to cure, providing a faster and more durable bond in high-temperature environments. Both types offer strong and fast bonding capabilities, making them popular choices for industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
