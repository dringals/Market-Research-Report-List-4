<p><ul><li>Single Pipetting Head</li><li>Dual Pipetting Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated liquid handling equipment comes in two main types: single pipetting head and dual pipetting head. Single pipetting head equipment has one arm/robot which can pipette one sample at a time, whereas dual pipetting head equipment has two arms/robots which can pipette two samples simultaneously. Single pipetting head systems are ideal for smaller laboratories with lower throughput needs, while dual pipetting head systems are better suited for larger laboratories with higher throughput requirements. Both types offer increased efficiency, accuracy, and reproducibility in liquid handling processes.</p></p>
