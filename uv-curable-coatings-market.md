<p><strong>The UV-Curable Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Base-coat</li><li>UV Mid-coat</li><li>UV Top-coat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV-Curable Coatings market can be categorized into three main types based on their application in the coating process: UV Base-coat, UV Mid-coat, and UV Top-coat. UV Base-coat is applied as the first layer on the substrate, providing adhesion and initial protection. UV Mid-coat is applied between the base-coat and top-coat to enhance the durability and aesthetic appeal of the coating. UV Top-coat is the final layer applied to provide additional protection, gloss, and finish to the coated surface. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the overall performance and appearance of the coated product.</p></p>
