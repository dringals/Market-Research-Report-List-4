<p><p>Veterinary ICU Cages come in two main types - Monoblock Type and Parallel Type. Monoblock Type cages are designed as a single unit with multiple compartments, allowing for easy cleaning and maintenance. Parallel Type cages have individual compartments that are stacked side by side, making it easier to access and observe each animal separately. Both types offer a secure and controlled environment for critically ill animals in veterinary clinics and hospitals, but differ in their design and layout.</p></p>
