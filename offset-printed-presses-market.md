<p><p>Offset printing presses are categorized into two main types: Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Press and Web-Fed Offset Printing Press. Sheet-Fed presses are designed for printing on individual sheets of paper, making them ideal for small to medium print runs and specialized printing such as packaging. On the other hand, Web-Fed presses are used for printing on continuous rolls of paper, making them more suitable for high-volume printing of newspapers, magazines, and catalogs. Both types of presses offer high-quality, cost-effective printing solutions for various industries.</p></p>
