<p><p>PCB solder resist ink is available in three main types - Photoimageable SRI, UV curable SRI, and Thermal curable SRI. Photoimageable SRI is applied using photolithography and offers high precision and resolution. UV curable SRI is cured using ultraviolet light, providing fast and efficient processing. Thermal curable SRI requires heat for curing and is known for its high chemical resistance. Each type of solder resist ink offers unique properties and benefits, catering to different requirements in the PCB manufacturing industry.</p></p>
