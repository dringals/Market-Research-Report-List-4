<p><strong>The Distributed Temperature Sensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multipoint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Temperature Sensing market is segmented into Single Point and Multipoint systems. Single Point systems use a single, fixed location sensor to measure temperature along a single point. On the other hand, Multipoint systems utilize multiple sensors placed along the fiber optic cable to measure temperature at multiple points simultaneously. Single Point systems are often used for simple temperature monitoring tasks while Multipoint systems are preferred for more complex applications that require monitoring temperature variations at different locations.</p></p>
