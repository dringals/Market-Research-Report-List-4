<p><p>Energy harvesting market includes various types such as photovoltaic, thermoelectric, piezo, and electrodynamic. Photovoltaic energy harvesting involves converting light into electricity using solar panels. Thermoelectric energy harvesting generates electricity from temperature differences. Piezo energy harvesting utilizes vibrations or mechanical stress to produce electricity. Electrodynamic energy harvesting converts mechanical movements into electrical energy. Each type of energy harvesting offers unique advantages and applications, catering to different environmental conditions and energy sources to harness sustainable energy for various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
