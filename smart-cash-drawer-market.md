<p><p>Smart cash drawers are a crucial component in the retail industry, with two main market types: All-In-One systems and POS touch machines. All-In-One systems integrate the cash drawer with a POS terminal, streamlining the checkout process and saving space. On the other hand, POS touch machines are standalone devices that can connect to a separate POS terminal. Both types offer security features like electronic locks and audit trails, as well as advanced functionalities such as bill validation and counterfeit detection.</p></p>
