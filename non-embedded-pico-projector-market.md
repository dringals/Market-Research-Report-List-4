<p><p>The non-embedded pico projector market includes Digital Light Processing (DLP), Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS), and Laser Beam Steering (LBS) technologies. DLP projectors use microscopic mirrors to create images, providing high contrast and brightness. LCoS projectors use liquid crystals to reflect light, offering high resolution and color accuracy. LBS projectors use lasers to steer beams and create images, resulting in vibrant colors and high efficiency. Each technology caters to different user preferences and applications in the pico projector market.</p></p>
