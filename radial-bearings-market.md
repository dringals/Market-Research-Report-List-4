<p><p>Radial bearings are commonly classified into three main types based on the material they are made of: metal, ceramic, and other. Metal radial bearings are typically made of steel and are known for their durability and strength. Ceramic radial bearings, on the other hand, are made of advanced ceramic materials like silicon nitride and offer advantages such as high temperature resistance and corrosion resistance. The "other" category includes bearings made of alternative materials like plastic or hybrid bearings made of a combination of materials.</p></p>
