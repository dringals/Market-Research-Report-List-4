<p><p>Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography (EUVL) Systems Market includes different types such as Laser Produced Plasmas, Vacuum Sparks, and Gas Discharges. Laser Produced Plasmas involve the use of high-energy lasers to create plasma which emits extreme ultraviolet light. Vacuum Sparks utilize high voltages to create sparks in a vacuum environment to produce EUV light. Gas Discharges involve the use of gases to generate plasma which then emits EUV light. Each market type has its own unique approach to producing extreme ultraviolet light for lithography systems.</p></p>
