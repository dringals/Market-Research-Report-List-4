<p><ul><li>Self-Propelled Belt Loader</li><li>Towed Belt Loader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-propelled belt loaders are designed with an engine that allows them to move independently around an airport or other loading area. They are ideal for quickly and efficiently loading and unloading baggage and cargo from aircraft. On the other hand, towed belt loaders need to be connected to a towing vehicle in order to be moved around. They are typically used for smaller aircraft or in situations where a self-propelled option is not necessary. Both types of belt loaders play a crucial role in the efficient operation of airports and other transportation hubs.</p></p>
