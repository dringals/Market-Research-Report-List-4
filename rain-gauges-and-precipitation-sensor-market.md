Tipping bucket rain gauges are equipped with a small bucket that fills with rainwater and tips over, recording each tip as a measurement of rainfall. Weighing type rain gauges measure rainfall by monitoring the weight of the collected water. Both types have their advantages and are widely used in various industries such as agriculture, meteorology, and research.</p></p>
