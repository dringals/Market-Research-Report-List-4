<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Hollow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Truck stabilizers are available in two main types: solid and hollow. Solid stabilizers are made from a solid piece of material, usually metal, and are known for their durability and strength. They provide excellent stability and weight distribution for trucks carrying heavy loads. On the other hand, hollow stabilizers are made from a hollow tube of material, which makes them lighter in weight. They are still strong and effective at stabilizing trucks but offer the advantage of reduced weight and improved fuel efficiency.</p></p>
