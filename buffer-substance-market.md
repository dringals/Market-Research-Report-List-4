<p><p>Buffer substances are chemicals that help maintain a stable pH level in solutions. There are two main types of buffer substances: acidic and alkaline. Acidic buffer substances work to resist changes in pH by absorbing excess hydrogen ions, while alkaline buffer substances resist changes in pH by absorbing excess hydroxide ions. Both types of buffer substances are crucial in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and water treatment, where maintaining specific pH levels is essential for the products or processes involved.</p></p>
