<p><ul><li>Single-mode Fiber Optical Switches</li><li>Multimode Fiber Optical Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optical Switches come in two main types - Single-mode and Multimode. Single-mode Fiber Optical Switches are designed for long-distance communication and high-speed data transmission. They use a narrower core to transmit light, resulting in less signal loss over longer distances. On the other hand, Multimode Fiber Optical Switches are used for shorter distances and slower data rates. They have a larger core size, allowing for easier coupling of light sources and receivers. Both types play crucial roles in modern telecommunications and data networks.</p></p>
