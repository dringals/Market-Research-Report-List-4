<p><strong>The Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monofocal Lenses</li><li>Bifocal Lenses</li><li>Trifocal Lenses</li><li>Progressive Multifocal Lenses</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monofocal lenses offer a single prescription power for distance or near vision. Bifocal lenses have two different prescriptions in one lens, typically for distance and near vision. Trifocal lenses have three prescriptions (for distance, intermediate, and near vision). Progressive multifocal lenses provide a gradual transition between different prescriptions, allowing for clear vision at all distances. Other types of lenses include toric lenses for astigmatism, photochromic lenses that darken in sunlight, and blue light blocking lenses for digital device users. Each type caters to specific vision needs.</p></p>
