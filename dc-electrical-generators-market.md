<p><strong>The DC Electrical Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Generators</li><li>Series Generators</li><li>Compound Wound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC electrical generators come in different types based on the way the field winding is connected to the armature. Shunt generators have the field winding connected in parallel to the armature, creating a constant voltage output. Series generators have the field winding connected in series with the armature, producing a high output with varying voltage. Compound wound generators have both shunt and series field windings, combining the advantages of both types for a more stable and efficient output. These different types cater to varying requirements in different applications.</p></p>
