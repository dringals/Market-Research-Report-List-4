<p><p>The plastic glove box market includes three main types: inert gas glove boxes, isolation glove boxes, and anaerobic glove boxes. Inert gas glove boxes are used for handling materials sensitive to oxygen or moisture, providing a controlled environment with an inert gas atmosphere. Isolation glove boxes are designed to protect users from hazardous materials and contamination. Anaerobic glove boxes create an oxygen-free environment for research or handling materials sensitive to oxygen. Each type of glove box serves a specific purpose in research, manufacturing, or material handling applications.</p></p>
