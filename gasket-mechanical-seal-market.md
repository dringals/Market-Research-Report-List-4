<p><p>The gasket market is divided into two main types: metallic and non-metallic. Metallic gaskets are made from materials like steel, copper, or aluminum and offer high strength and durability, making them ideal for applications with high pressure and temperature requirements. Non-metallic gaskets, on the other hand, are made from materials like rubber, silicone, or graphite and provide flexibility and chemical resistance, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications. Both types of gaskets play a crucial role in ensuring tight seals in mechanical systems.</p></p>
