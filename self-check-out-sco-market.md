<p><p>Self-Check-out (SCO) systems come in three main market types: stand-alone, wall-mounted, and countertop. Stand-alone SCO systems are separate terminals placed in an aisle for customers to use. Wall-mounted SCO systems are mounted on a wall near the entrance or exit of a store for convenience. Countertop SCO systems are placed on a checkout counter for customers to use while checking out. Each market type offers a different level of accessibility and convenience for customers to use while shopping.</p></p>
