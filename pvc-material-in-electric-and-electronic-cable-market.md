Two main methods for producing PVC are the Calcium Carbide method and the Ethylene method. The Calcium Carbide method involves reacting calcium carbide with acetylene to produce PVC, while the Ethylene method involves polymerizing vinyl chloride monomers obtained from ethylene gas. Both methods play a significant role in the market for PVC materials used in electric and electronic cables.</p></p>
