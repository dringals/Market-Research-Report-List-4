<p><p>Vertical-cavity surface emitting lasers (VCSELs) can be classified into two main market types based on their emission behavior: single-mode VCSELs and multimode VCSELs. Single-mode VCSELs emit a single, narrow beam of light, making them suitable for high-speed data communication applications. On the other hand, multimode VCSELs emit multiple beams of light simultaneously, allowing for increased power output and broader coverage, making them ideal for applications such as sensing, imaging, and industrial applications. Both types of VCSELs have their own unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
