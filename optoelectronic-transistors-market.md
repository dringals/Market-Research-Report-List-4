<p><ul><li>Tank Enclosed Type</li><li>Resin Enclosed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optoelectronic transistors are classified into two main types: Tank enclosed type and Resin enclosed type. Tank enclosed type transistors have a robust metal casing that provides protection against external elements and physical damage, making them ideal for harsh environments. On the other hand, Resin enclosed type transistors are encased in a durable resin material that offers superior insulation and moisture resistance. Both types cater to different application needs, ensuring reliability and performance in various operating conditions.</p></p>
