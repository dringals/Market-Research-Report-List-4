<p><p>Extracorporeal blood treatment market includes Hemodialysis, Acute dialysis, and Apheresis, which are essential procedures for removing waste and toxins from the blood outside of the body. Hemodialysis is a common treatment for patients with kidney failure, while acute dialysis is used for those with sudden kidney injury. Apheresis involves separating and removing specific components of the blood, such as plasma or white blood cells. These procedures play a crucial role in maintaining proper blood function and overall health for patients with renal and other medical conditions.</p></p>
