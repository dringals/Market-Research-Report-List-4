<p><p>Paper Pulper market types include Hydrapulper, Drum Hydrapulper, and Broke Pulper. Hydrapulper is a high consistency pulping equipment used for breaking and removing impurities from recycled paper. Drum Hydrapulper is another type of high consistency pulper that uses a rotating drum to break down paper. Broke Pulper is specifically designed for processing broke paper (waste paper or trimmings) in paper mills. Each type of pulper serves a specific purpose in the recycling and processing of paper materials.</p></p>
