<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Cleaning Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suction-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Pressure-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Electric Pool Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic pool cleaning machines come in various types to suit different pool cleaning needs. Suction-side pool cleaners are connected to the skimmer or suction line and use the pool's filtration system to collect debris. Pressure-side pool cleaners use the pressure from the pool's return line to move around and collect debris. Electric pool cleaners are powered by electricity and operate independently from the pool's filtration system. Each type of automatic pool cleaner offers unique features and benefits to keep pools clean and well-maintained.</p></p>
