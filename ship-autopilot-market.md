<p><p>The ship autopilot market can be categorized into three main types based on functionality: automatic autopilots, follow-up autopilots, and manual autopilots. Automatic autopilots utilize sensors and algorithms to navigate the ship without human intervention. Follow-up autopilots allow for manual adjustments to the autopilot's course. Manual autopilots require constant manual input from a human operator to steer the ship. Each type caters to different levels of autonomy and control, providing options for various ship navigation needs.</p></p>
