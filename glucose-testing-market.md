<p><p>Glucose testing market includes two main types: minimal invasive testing and non-invasive testing. Minimal invasive testing involves the use of devices that require a small amount of blood to measure glucose levels, such as lancets and glucose meters. On the other hand, non-invasive testing methods eliminate the need for blood samples and instead use technologies like sensors or breath analysis to measure glucose levels. Both types offer convenient and efficient solutions for monitoring glucose levels in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
