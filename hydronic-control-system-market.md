<p><p>Hydronic control system market types can be categorized into actuators, valves, flow controllers, control panels, and others. Actuators are responsible for regulating the flow of water in the system, while valves control the direction and volume of water. Flow controllers help maintain a consistent flow rate, and control panels manage the overall operation of the system. Other components may include sensors, thermostats, and pumps. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring efficient and effective control of hydronic heating and cooling systems.</p></p>
