<p><p>Hydraulic Gear Wheel Pump market includes two main types - External Gear Hydraulic Gear Wheel Pump and Internal Gear Hydraulic Gear Wheel Pump. External Gear pumps have two gears that rotate in opposite directions to pump fluid, while Internal Gear pumps have an inner and outer gear that mesh together to pump fluid. External Gear pumps are known for their high efficiency and suitability for high-pressure applications, while Internal Gear pumps are favored for their quiet operation and ability to handle high speeds.</p></p>
