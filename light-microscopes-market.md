<p><p>Light microscopes are available in three types based on the number of eyepieces they have: monocular, binocular, and trinocular. Monocular microscopes have a single eyepiece, making them more compact and lightweight, ideal for individual use. Binocular microscopes feature two eyepieces, providing a more comfortable viewing experience for longer periods. Trinocular microscopes have three eyepieces, with the additional eyepiece typically used for attaching a camera or digital imaging device for capturing and analyzing images.</p></p>
