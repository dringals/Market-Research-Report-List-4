<p><p>Point of care hematology analyzers come in various types, with the main differentiator being the type of differentiation they provide. 3-part differentiation analyzers classify blood cells into three main categories: red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets. On the other hand, 5-part differentiation analyzers provide a more detailed analysis by differentiating between various types of white blood cells, such as neutrophils, lymphocytes, and monocytes. This additional differentiation can provide clinicians with more comprehensive information about a patient's health status.</p></p>
