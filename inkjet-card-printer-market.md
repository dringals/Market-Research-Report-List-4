<p><p>The Inkjet Card Printer market consists of two main types: Thermal Foaming and Micro Piezo. Thermal Foaming technology uses heat to create tiny droplets of ink, resulting in high-resolution prints. On the other hand, Micro Piezo technology propels ink droplets onto the paper using electrical signals, allowing for precise and accurate printing. Both technologies offer different advantages depending on the specific needs of the user, such as speed, quality, and cost-effectiveness. Overall, the Inkjet Card Printer market caters to a wide range of applications with these two distinct types of technology.</p></p>
