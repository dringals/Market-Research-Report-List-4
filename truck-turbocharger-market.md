<p><p>Truck turbocharger market offers two types: Mono Turbo and Twin Turbo. Mono Turbo system uses a single turbocharger to increase engine performance by delivering compressed air to the engine, while Twin Turbo system utilizes two turbochargers for enhanced power output. Mono Turbo is more common in trucks due to its simpler design and cost-effectiveness, while Twin Turbo is typically found in high-performance trucks for superior performance. Both types aim to enhance engine efficiency and power output for better truck performance.</p></p>
