<p><p>Bottle washing machines can be categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines are fully automated, with sensors and timers controlling the washing process. They are more efficient and require less human intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual input, such as loading and unloading bottles, but still have automated washing cycles. They are typically more cost-effective for smaller-scale operations. Both types of machines cater to different market segments based on their level of automation and efficiency.</p></p>
