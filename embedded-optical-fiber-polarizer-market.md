<p><p>Embedded Optical Fiber Polarizers are devices used to control the polarization of light within optical fiber systems. They come in two main types: SM Type and PM Type. </p><p>SM Type polarizers are designed to work with single-mode fibers and are used to control the polarization state of light traveling through the fiber. </p><p>PM Type polarizers, on the other hand, are specifically designed for use with polarization-maintaining fibers and provide precise control over the polarization of light to maintain the desired polarization state throughout the fiber system.</p></p>
