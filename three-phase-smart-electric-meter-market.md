<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) smart electric meters use existing electrical wiring to communicate data between the meter and the utility company, offering a cost-effective solution for utilities. Radio Frequency (RF) meters utilize wireless communication to transmit data, providing flexibility and ease of installation in areas where PLC may not be feasible. Other types of smart meters may include cellular or Ethernet-based communication methods, offering a wide range of options for utilities to choose from based on their specific needs and infrastructure.</p></p>
