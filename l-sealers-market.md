<p><p>L-Sealers are packaging machines used to wrap products, typically using heat shrink film. The market for L-Sealers includes three main types: automatic, semi-automatic, and manual machines. Automatic L-Sealers are fully automated and require minimal human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production. Semi-automatic L-Sealers require some manual loading and unloading of products but have automated sealing and cutting functions. Manual L-Sealers are operated entirely by hand, making them suitable for small-scale operations or businesses with limited production requirements.</p></p>
