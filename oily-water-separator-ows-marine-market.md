<p><p>Oily Water Separator (OWS) in the marine industry is utilized to separate oil and other contaminants from water before disposing of it into the sea. There are several types of OWS in the market including Gravity plate separator, Electrochemical, Bioremediation, and Centrifugal. Gravity plate separator uses the difference in density to separate oil and water, Electrochemical utilizes electric current to separate contaminants, Bioremediation uses microorganisms to break down pollutants, and centrifugal technology uses centrifugal force to separate oil and water.</p></p>
