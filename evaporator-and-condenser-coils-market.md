Tubed coils consist of a series of tubes carrying refrigerant, while finned/plated coils have additional fins or plates for enhanced heat transfer. Combined coils incorporate features of both tubed and finned/plated coils. Each type of coil offers different benefits and is used in various applications based on specific requirements for cooling systems.</p></p>
