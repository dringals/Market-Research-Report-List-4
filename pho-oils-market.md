This trend is driving the food industry to reformulate their products and switch to healthier oil options, further propelling the growth of the PHO oils market. Additionally, government regulations banning the use of PHO oils in food products are also contributing to the market growth as manufacturers are forced to find suitable replacements.</p></p>
