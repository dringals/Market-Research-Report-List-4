<p><ul><li>Isolated Chip</li><li>Non-Isolated Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Interface Chip market can be divided into two main types: Isolated Chip and Non-Isolated Chip. Isolated chips have a built-in isolation barrier to protect the circuitry from external noise and ensure accurate signal transmission. On the other hand, Non-Isolated chips do not have this barrier and are typically used in applications where noise interference is not a concern. Both types play a crucial role in improving vehicle performance and connectivity by enabling seamless communication between different components in the automotive system.</p></p>
