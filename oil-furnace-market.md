They are typically more expensive upfront but provide long-term cost savings. Oil Fired Indirect Heaters utilize a boiler to heat water, which is then circulated through coils in the heater to transfer heat to the surrounding air. They are ideal for larger spaces or commercial applications where high BTU output is required. Both types offer efficient heating solutions for homes and businesses.</p></p>
