<p><p>Side channel pumps are classified into two market types - Type I and Type II. Type I pumps are designed for low flow rates and high pressures, making them suitable for applications such as boiler feed systems and fuel transfer. On the other hand, Type II pumps are built for high flow rates and low to medium pressures, making them ideal for applications like water circulation and irrigation. Both types offer efficient and reliable performance in their respective market segments.</p></p>
