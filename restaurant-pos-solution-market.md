<p><p>Restaurant POS solutions are available in three main market types: cloud-based, on-premise, and hybrid. Cloud-based systems operate entirely online, providing flexibility and accessibility from any location. On-premise systems are installed locally on a restaurant's hardware, offering more control over data but requiring higher upfront costs. Hybrid solutions combine elements of both cloud-based and on-premise systems, allowing for a blend of flexibility and control. Each market type caters to different restaurant needs and preferences.</p></p>
