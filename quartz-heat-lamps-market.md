<p><p>Quartz heat lamps are classified into three main types based on their wavelength emissions: short wave, medium wave, and long wave. Short wave lamps produce intense heat quickly, making them ideal for processes requiring high temperatures in a short amount of time. Medium wave lamps provide a balance between heating speed and efficiency, suitable for a wide range of applications. Long wave lamps emit gentle, consistent heat, making them ideal for slow and steady heating processes where precision is key. Each type has its own advantages and is utilized in various industries based on specific heating requirements.</p></p>
